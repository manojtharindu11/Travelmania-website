Kataragama Esala Festival



One of the most significant Hindu festivals in Sri Lanka, to the holy town of Kataragama in southern Sri Lanka over a two-week period. People from all major faiths seek divine guidance and penance from the deity by indulging in rituals like skin-piercing and acts of self-mutilation. Just like the Esala Perahera, this festival is also marked by processions of dancers, musicians, elephants, fire-eaters and acrobats, all adding up to the religious fervor and festivities.

  • When: 21 Jul, 2022
  • Where: Kataragama town in Southern Sri Lanka
  • Highlight Of The Festival: Performance by fire-eaters and acrobats
